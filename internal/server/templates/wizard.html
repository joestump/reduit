Sign in to your Proton account

Reduit never stores your Proton password. We exchange it for a long-lived refresh token and discard the password from memory.

Use your Proton login password, not your mailbox key.

{{end}} {{define "wizard_step_totp"}}

Enter your two-factor code

Open your authenticator app (1Password, Authy, Aegis, etc.) and enter the 6-digit code for protonmail.com.

Codes refresh every 30 seconds. Three failed attempts will end this wizard.

{{end}} {{define "wizard_step_unlock"}}

Unlock your Proton mailbox

Proton encrypts your mail with a separate mailbox key. Reduit needs your mailbox passphrase to decrypt messages locally on the relay. If you have never set a separate one, this is the same as your Proton login password.

We encrypt this passphrase at rest with the operator's master key. It is never written to logs or transmitted off this host.

Same as your login password unless you set a separate mailbox key in Proton settings.
